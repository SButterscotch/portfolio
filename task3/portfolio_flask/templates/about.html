About Me

I am Sambhav Karki, currently a junior majoring in Computer Science at the University of Idaho. I have a passion for creating video games, and I design all my characters and environments, drawing from my love of art and mythology. In addition to game development, I have experience in infrastructure, urban development, and have built several personal projects. My current endeavor is BarterDB for this class (CS 360), a secure anonymous barter system aimed at transforming how people exchange goods online.

My journey began in Nepal, where I was born and spent most of my life. This rich cultural background inspired me to incorporate unique elements into my work. I enjoy combining technology and design to create immersive experiences that challenge traditional narratives. As I continue my studies, I aspire to work on larger projects that merge engineering and art in meaningful ways.
